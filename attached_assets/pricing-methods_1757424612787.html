All incoming data passes through automated validation checks including range validation, consistency checks, and anomaly detection. Invalid data points are flagged and excluded from analysis.

Model Performance Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of model performance with automated alerts for accuracy degradation. Models are retrained monthly or when performance drops below 90% accuracy threshold.

Statistical Significance

All reported metrics include confidence intervals and statistical significance testing. Only results with p-value < 0.05 are considered statistically significant.
