Compliance & Ethics

Blaze Intelligence operates with the highest standards of data ethics and compliance. We maintain strict adherence to NCAA regulations, professional league guidelines, and data privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA. Our platform requires explicit consent for any biometric or image-based analysis.

We are committed to protecting Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) rights for all athletes and ensure that our valuation algorithms are transparent, fair, and free from bias. All data processing is performed with appropriate consent and in accordance with applicable regulations.
