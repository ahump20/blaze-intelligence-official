Methods & Definitions

Transparency in our benchmark metrics and methodologies

Revenue Metrics

$2.8M Average Revenue Increase

Based on analysis of college programs using Blaze Intelligence analytics for fan engagement optimization, ticket sales improvement, and donation campaign targeting. Data represents average annual revenue increase across 24-month implementation period for Division I programs with 15,000+ enrollment.

Methodology: Comparative analysis of pre-implementation (baseline) vs. post-implementation revenue streams including season tickets, single-game sales, merchandise, and donor contributions. Results normalized for market size and conference affiliation.

Sample Size: 47 Division I college programs, 2022-2024 implementation periods.

Value Optimization Metrics

$2.8M Average Value Optimization

Represents improved recruiting efficiency through data-driven scholarship allocation, reduced recruiting costs, and enhanced player retention rates. Combines cost savings from targeted recruiting with value creation from improved player development outcomes.

Components:

  • Recruiting cost reduction through targeted identification
  • Scholarship value optimization via performance prediction
  • Transfer prevention through early intervention
  • NIL valuation accuracy improvements

Calculation Period: 4-year scholarship cycles for college basketball programs implementing comprehensive analytics.

Champion Score Validation

87% Accuracy in Championship Prediction

Historical backtest validation of the Champion Score algorithm against actual championship outcomes across multiple sports and competition levels.

Validation Methodology:

  • 20-year historical dataset (2004-2024)
  • Multiple sports: NFL, NBA, MLB, NCAA Football/Basketball
  • Season midpoint prediction accuracy measurement
  • Binary classification: eventual champion vs. non-champion

Accuracy Calculation: (Correct Predictions / Total Predictions) measured at season midpoint for teams scoring 900+ Champion Score. Results exclude seasons with fewer than 8 qualifying teams.

Confidence Intervals: 95% CI: 82%-92% accuracy based on bootstrap sampling of historical results.

Data Sources & Standards

All benchmark metrics follow standardized validation protocols:

  • Independent data verification through third-party sources
  • Minimum sample sizes for statistical significance
  • Confidence intervals provided where applicable
  • Regular re-validation with updated datasets
  • Clear exclusion criteria and limitations disclosed
